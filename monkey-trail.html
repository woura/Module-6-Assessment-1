MONKEY TRAIL

monkey trail (plural monkey trails) (western Canada) A beaten path where children play. The Monkey Trail is a series of loop trails throughout 800+ acres of mature hardwood forest at Eddie D Jones Park.Open dawn to dusk every day.. The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round.
